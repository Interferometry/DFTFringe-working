DFTFringe Analysis Step by Step

Step 1.

Make an igram with as much tilt as you can get before the fringes blur out. First focus the camera on a target placed at the surface of the mirror. That will insure a well defined edge of the mirror in the interferogram.



Step 2.

Enter your mirror parameters and laser wavelength into the the mirror configuartion dialog.



Step 3.

Load an interferogram, check and adjust the mirror outline if necessary then press "Done"


Step 4.

Adjust the blue center sfilter and press "compute surface"


Step 5.

View results

Inspect the analysis and check for unwrap errors.

Step 6.

Improve the accuracy. Take several interferograms at several mirror rotations, counter rotate the results and average all together.
